About me

I'm a final-year Computer Science & Engineering student at East West University with a major in AI & Machine Learning. My work spans production-ready SaaS platforms, cross-platform mobile apps, headless automation agents, and applied NLP research.

"Translating intricate business logic into secure, high-performance, user-centric digital products."

On the engineering side, I architect with Next.js, the MERN stack, Supabase, and PostgreSQL — recent shipped work includes MealStack (real-time inventory + waste analytics), Medico (cross-platform healthcare app), and a WhatsApp automation agent for residential mess management. I'm currently leading the technical architecture of SkillSwap BD, a time-banking skill-exchange platform.

On the research side, my capstone thesis investigates a sarcasm-aware framework for detecting implicit hate speech in Bangla and English — using XLM-RoBERTa with dual-head architectures on several datasets.
